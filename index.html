INSURANCE CLAIM TERMS
This Agreement is contingent upon approval of the insurance claim by Customer’s insurance company. Trophy Roofing & Construction will perform the work in accordance with the insurance company’s approved damage and work scope.

Customer’s out-of-pocket expense shall not exceed their applicable deductible, provided the insurance policy releases full Replacement Cost Value (RCV). If the policy pays Actual Cash Value (ACV) only and depreciation is not released to the Customer, Trophy Roofing & Construction is not obligated to perform the work at the deductible-only price and reserves the right to re-price the project or cancel this Agreement without penalty to either party.

In the rare circumstance that the insurance company’s approved RCV is insufficient to cover the actual cost of the work at Trophy’s standard rates, Trophy Roofing & Construction reserves the right to respectfully decline to perform the work. In such cases, Trophy will notify the Customer promptly and assist in exploring available options, and this Agreement shall be cancelled without penalty to either party.

Supplements: In situations where supplements for additional work are necessary outside of the original approved scope (e.g., additional layers, mismeasurements, code upgrades), Trophy will seek approval from the insurance company. Customer authorizes Trophy to communicate directly with the insurance company and adjuster on their behalf.

Texas Deductible Law Disclosure (Required by Texas Law): Texas law requires a person insured under a property insurance policy to pay any deductible applicable to a claim made under the policy. It is a violation of Texas law for a contractor paid wholly or partly from insurance proceeds to knowingly allow the insured to fail to pay, or to assist the insured’s failure to pay, the applicable insurance deductible. See Texas Business & Commerce Code §27.02.
